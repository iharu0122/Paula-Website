BHA Kit for Face & Body – Clearer, Smoother Skin Everywhere

Say goodbye to clogged pores, rough texture, and body breakouts. Our BHA Kit for Face & Body is designed to exfoliate, clear, and smooth your skin from head to toe. Powered by Beta Hydroxy Acid (salicylic acid), it gently removes dead skin cells, unclogs pores, and helps calm redness without irritation.

The face treatment targets blackheads, enlarged pores, and uneven texture, while the body formula tackles body acne, rough patches, and keratosis pilaris (tiny bumps). Both products hydrate and soothe as they work, leaving your skin softer, clearer, and more even-toned.

Targets: Rough, bumpy skin, Clogged pores, Body acne, Uneven skin texture
Format: Kit with 1× 210ml Body Treatment + 1× 118ml BHA Liquid Exfoliant.
Suited For: All skin types including sensitive and keratosis pilaris-prone skin.
Key Ingredients: Salicylic Acid (BHA), Green Tea Extract, Glycerin, Allantoin.
